Delete Account

⚠️ Warning: This action cannot be undone!

Deleting your account will permanently deactivate it. You will lose access to all your data, masjid management permissions, and account information.

What happens when you delete your account:
  • Your account will be deactivated
  • You will lose access to all masajids you manage
  • You will no longer receive notifications
  • All your account data will be marked as inactive
  • You will need to contact support to reactivate your account

Alternative: If you prefer to delete your account from within the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the AlAsr Manager app.
